The Last of Us

The Last of Us Image: The Last of Us wallpaper by Rob Obsidian at https://www.flickr.com/photos/65092514@N08/26312291165, CC BY 2.0.

The Last of Us is my favorite single-player video game of all time. Even though it may just be video game on the outside, it is, in all honesty, a storytelling masterpieces that even some modern day films or TV shows can't even compare to.

It is a story when you play as a character Joel, a smuggler tasked with escorting a teenage girl, Ellie, across a post-apocalyptic United States.

While from the description, it doesn't seem all that interesting, you get to fight off zombies referred to as infected as well as getting to witness the stuggles humans have with each other in such times. You also get to enjoy the bond the two protagonists, Joel and Ellie, form as they try to survive and protect each other throught the many hardships that they face
