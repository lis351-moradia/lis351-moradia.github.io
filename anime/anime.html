One Piece's Description

It is the story of Monkey D. Luffy, a young man who has a single dream: To find the legendary treasure known as the One Piece and become the King of the Pirates
Alongside a crew of trusted friends, Luffy sails the dangerous seas of the Grand Line to find Laugh Tale, the hidden island containing the One Piece

One Piece's Appeals

Lovable Characters
Fantastic Character Building and their Individual Arcs
A Fun and at Times Tear-Evoking Plot
The Different Adventures the Crew Goes On are Enjoyable
Main Character's Perserverance

Fullmetal Alchemist

Fullmetal Alchemist Image: Edward and Alphonse Elric wallpaper by valiant ho at https://www.flickr.com/photos/toysworkz/2037918079, CC BY-NC-ND 2.0.

Fullmetal Alchemist's Description

Set in the early 20th century, in a fictional universe in which alchemy is a widely practiced science, the series follows the journey of two alchemist brothers, Edward and Alphonse Elric, who are searching for the philosopher's stone to restore their bodies after a failed attempt to bring their mother back to life using alchemy

Fullmetal Alchemist's Appeals

Plot Twists
Phenomenal Storyline
Great Characters and Relationships, especially between the two brothers
Great Pacing
