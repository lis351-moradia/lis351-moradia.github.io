The Dark Knight

Dark Knight Image: The Dark Knight wallpaper by lindseo at https://www.flickr.com/photos/47239172@N00/3207528353, CC BY 2.0.

The Dark Knight is probably my favorite movie of all-time. Even though it just be another Batman film, let me list out a few reason why I enjoy this movie so much:

  • The Acting (especially by Christian Bale and Heath Ledger)
  • The dark vibes and tone the movie gave out just make me feel some type of way that other movies haven't been able to accomplish
  • Let's not forget about the opening bank robbery scene
  • All of the Joker's scenes were just simply amazing
  • Overall, the storytelling and acting is so enjoyable to watch
